FAQ

Where can I register for COVID-19 vaccination?
You can log into the CoWIN portal and click on the “Register” tab to register for COVID-19 vaccination.
Which age groups can register for vaccination on the VAX-WIN portal?
All citizens aged 18 years and above can register for vaccination.
Can I register for vaccination without Aadhaar card?
Yes, you can register on CoWIN portal using any of the following ID proofs:
  • Aadhaar card
  • Driving License
  • PAN card
  • Passport
  • Voter ID (EPIC)
Is there any registration charges to be paid?
No. There is no registration charge.
Whom do I contact in case of side effects from vaccination?
You can contact on any of the following details:
  • Helpline Number: +91-11-23978046 (Toll free - 1075)
  • Helpline Number: 0120-4473222
  • You may also contact the Vaccination Centre where you took vaccination, for advice.
